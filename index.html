Bailey Cooper

| Writer |

Bailey Cooper is an award-winning author of fiction and nonfiction, known for her evocative storytelling and deep engagement with the human condition. Her work has been published in numerous international journals and anthologies.

With a background in journalism and literature, Bailey blends research with creativity to produce compelling narratives. She is passionate about mentoring emerging writers and often appears at festivals and workshops around the globe.
