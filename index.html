Bailey Cooper

| Writer and Poet |

Bailey Cooper is a writer and experimental poet working in Naarm (Melbourne). His work is primarily nonfiction albeit opaque in nature. He is preoccupied with spaces and the time that allows for them.

He has a background in mathematics and science having previously studied engineering. He aims to bring the exactness and specificity of these fields into his writing as a way to bring clarity and definition to the things that would go otherwise unsaid.

Currently, he is at work on his first full collection of poems.
